Welcome to our Meal Prep App
  • Please log in to get started.
    • If there was no pop-up, please click the icon to login with a valid Gmail.
    • Use the tabs on the upper left to navigate around the app.

  • Search Recipes: Enter a keyword and optional diet and cuisine modifiers to look up a recipe.
    • You can view the specific details of a recipe by clicking on the "View Recipe Details" button.
    • In the recipe details, you can choose to add the recipe to your favorites if you are logged in.

  • Planner
    • Inside the planner is a list of your favorited recipes and a calendar for meal planning
    • Once again you can view the recipe details, but here you can also add specific or all of ingredients to your grocery cart.
    • You can also remove the recipe from your favorites by clicking "Delete from Favorites".
